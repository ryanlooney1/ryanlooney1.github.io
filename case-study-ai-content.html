Overview

I introduced system prompt writing as a new content design capability, helping shape how AI-generated product content is created and evaluated.

Problem

Engineers owned the prompt language throughout. I identified an opportunity to apply content expertise at scale to improve output quality and model performance and to define how content designers could contribute to AI systems.

Approach

I persuaded leadership and partnered with engineering and design teams to introduce system prompt writing as a content design responsibility. I developed evaluation frameworks, created a playbook, and hosted hands-on learning sessions to help other content designers apply these practices.

Impact

This helped establish content design as a core contributor to AI product development, supported the evolution of the content design discipline into AI systems work, and improved collaboration between design and engineering around AI content quality.
