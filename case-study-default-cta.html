Challenge

Ad CTAs are the single most-important content across all of Meta, with billions of daily impressions driving the bulk of Meta's revenue. But when I started investigating, I discovered that no team at Meta formally owned the list of available CTAs. "Learn More" had become the default not by design, but by neglect. It was everywhere because no one had looked hard enough to change it.

I recognized this as a significant untapped opportunity. If even a small improvement to the default CTA increased advertiser performance, the impact at Meta's scale would be enormous.

"Most valuable thought partner throughout this initiative. Wouldn't be here without you."
Product Manager, Instagram Business
Approach
1. Found who owned it (nobody)

I mapped ownership across monetization, product, and content teams and confirmed the gap was real. Establishing accountability was the first step to making change possible.

2. Built and ran a testing program

I created and tested 1,000+ CTA variants across Instagram ad surfaces to identify what language actually drove better advertiser outcomes at scale.

3. Aligned leadership and launched

I brought findings to leadership across Meta's monetization teams, built the case for a new default, and helped drive the first new default ad CTA to launch.

Impact
  • Delivered Meta's first new default ad CTA
  • Launched dynamic CTAs giving Instagram advertisers more relevant, specific options
  • Generated measurable improvements in advertiser dwell time, clicks, and performance
  • Contributed to an estimated $200M+ in revenue impact

Key insight

The biggest opportunities hide in plain sight beneath the unchallenged status quo.

What I'd do differently: Next time I find a gap this size, I'll move faster to formalize ownership and staff it properly from the start.
